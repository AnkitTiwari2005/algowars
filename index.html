THE BATTLEFIELD

ALGOWAR – The Battle of Ideas is an exciting two-round ideathon aimed at fostering creativity and innovation among participants. Transform your ideas into impactful solutions and showcase your talent and problem-solving skills.

IDEATION

Teams will be given problem statements to develop innovative ideas or prototypes. Evaluators will assess based on creativity, feasibility, and technical execution.

PRESENTATION

Top 10 teams will present their ideas through PPTs before a panel of expert jury members. Winners will be honored with awards and recognition.

VICTORY

Compete for amazing rewards and recognition from CSI and industry partners. Showcase your talent and win the ultimate battle of ideas.

BATTLE INTEL

Who can participate in AlgoWars?

AlgoWars is open to all students of Chandigarh University. Teams should consist of 2-3 members from any year or discipline.

Do I need to bring anything?

Laptops are mandatory for all participants. You should also bring your student ID for verification and a notebook for brainstorming.

How will the ideas be evaluated?

Ideas will be evaluated based on:

  • Innovation and creativity
  • Feasibility and practicality
  • Potential impact
  • Quality of presentation (for final round)

Will there be internet access?

Yes, internet access will be available for research and development during the ideation round.
